Our Role

Habitat for Humanity of Waldo County is a local Habitat for Humanity International affiliate dedicated to the belief that all people deserve decent, safe, and affordable housing on terms they can afford. We build simple, energy-efficient homes for Waldo Countyâ€™s eligible applicants with a spirit of hope and unity. The essence of Habitat for Humanity of Waldo County is inviting diverse individuals to find common ground through using a hammer and sharing a goal. We build relationships as well as houses, along with the pride in service, homeownership, and community.

Our Mission

Putting God's love into action, Habitat for Humanity of Waldo County seeks to build strength, stability, self-sufficiency, and shelter.

Our Vision

A world in which everyone has a decent place to live.
